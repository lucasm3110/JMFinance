  • Non-Conventional Loans (Jumbo, HELOC, Reverse Mortgage)

JM3 Finance offers a range of non-conventional mortgage loan options to meet diverse real estate financing needs. These loans cater to specific situations or borrower profiles that may not fit within traditional mortgage programs. Let's explore three types of non-conventional mortgage loans: Jumbo loans, Home Equity Lines of Credit (HELOC), and Reverse mortgages.

Jumbo Loans: Jumbo loans are designed for borrowers who require financing beyond the limits set by conventional loan programs. JM3 Finance can provide jumbo loans that exceed the loan limits set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. These loans are typically used for high-value properties or when the borrower needs to borrow a larger amount to finance their real estate purchase.

Home Equity Lines of Credit (HELOC): HELOCs allow homeowners to access the equity in their homes as a line of credit. JM3 Finance offers HELOCs, which allow borrowers to tap into their home equity and use it for various purposes, such as home improvements, debt consolidation, or investment opportunities. With a HELOC, borrowers have the flexibility to draw funds as needed and only pay interest on the amount used.

Reverse Mortgages: Reverse mortgages are specifically designed for senior homeowners aged 62 and older. JM3 Finance can assist eligible borrowers in accessing the equity in their homes to supplement retirement income, pay for healthcare expenses, or meet other financial needs. With a reverse mortgage, borrowers receive loan proceeds based on the equity in their home, and repayment is typically deferred until the borrower sells the property or no longer occupies it as their primary residence.

We tailor loan solutions that align with their specific requirements, whether it's a jumbo loan for a high-value property, a HELOC for accessing home equity, or a reverse mortgage for seniors.
